Why do A-players choose to work with us?

All of us hold Computer Science and Engineering degrees from top universities in the US and in India. Many staff members have earned advanced degrees in Computer Science and management. In addition, we complete required Microsoft and Amazon Web Services certifications every year to stay technically proficient. Our company has the highest density in the industry of Agile Certified Practitioners (ACP) from the Project Management Institute (PMI).
